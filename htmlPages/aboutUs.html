About Us

We are Sandra (Celine) Jammal and Hossen Yassen, passionate software engineering students. As part of our academic journey, we developed a JavaScript-based project called Phone Book, which helps users organize and manage their contacts with ease.

Our Mission

Our goal is to build intuitive and user-friendly applications that solve real-world problems. We believe in continuous learning and collaborating to create meaningful, efficient solutions that can make everyday tasks simpler and more organized.

Our Journey

As students, we have tackled various challenges, developed skills in coding and problem-solving, and worked on diverse projects. We are excited to share Phone Book as one of our milestones and are constantly working to improve it and grow as developers.
